About

BloomWatch is designed by Snorita Asrafee 🌸. It uses NASA GLOBE Phenology data and MODIS NDVI to visualize blooms, predict flowering events, and highlight vegetation hotspots with a modern eco-space interface. Interactive charts and maps allow you to track flowering trends and hotspots in real-time.
